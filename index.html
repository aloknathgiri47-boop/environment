Our Mission

We Fight for a Greener World Every Day

EarthGuard is a global environmental non-profit dedicated to reversing ecological damage, conserving biodiversity, and empowering communities to live sustainably. Since 2008, we've turned grassroots passion into measurable change.

Our science-backed programs address the root causes of climate change, deforestation, ocean pollution, and species extinction. Every dollar you donate is put to work in the field — not in boardrooms.
